Privacy Policy

About this policy

This Privacy Policy (referred to as "Policy") describes how we process your personal data at Keasbey Company LLC (referred to as "Keasbey Company") and applies to the user data on the website with the domain https://www.yoon-dev.com and its subdomains. The Policy applies to the user's access of the website and its content as well as the access and use of Keasbey Company applications (referred to as "Keasbey Services").

The privacy policy is a separate document distinct from the Terms of Use, of which the latter is a legally binding contract between you and Keasbey Company and outlines the user rights and obligations. This Policy outlines key information about your personal data. Keasbey Company complies with different data privacy norms and regulations, including the EU data privacy regulations.

Cookies policy

What are cookies?

Cookies are files saved on your phone, tablet or computer when you visit a website. For mobile apps we use so-called browser trackers instead of cookies, but they have the same function as cookies. In this document we use the term "Cookies" for both cookies as well as trackers.

This website uses so-called “technical” cookies and other similar trackers to carry out activities that are strictly necessary for the operation or delivery of the Keasbey Service. Some of the purposes for which trackers are used may also require your consent. Whenever consent is given, it can be freely withdrawn at any time by deleting your browser data.

This website uses cookies managed directly by us (so-called “first-party” cookies) and cookies that enable services provided by a third-party (so-called “third-party” cookies). Unless otherwise specified within this document, third-party providers may access the cookies managed by them.

Displaying content from external platforms

This type of service allows you to view content hosted on external platforms directly from the pages of this website and interact with them. Such services are often referred to as widgets, which are small elements placed on a website or app. They provide specific information or perform a particular function and often allow for user interaction. This type of service might still collect web traffic data for the pages where the service is installed, even when you do not use it.

Google Fonts and Analytics (Google LLC)

Google Fonts is a typeface visualization service provided by Google LLC that allows this website to incorporate content of this kind on its pages. Keasbey Company does not directly retrieve data from the Google fonts services and instead directly hosts Google fonts (Roboto) on our servers. However, Keasbey Company uses bootstrap for styling, which might access Google fonts indirectly.

Google Analytics (Universal Analytics) is a web analysis service provided by Google LLC (“Google”). We use Google Analytics to analyze user and usage data. Google utilizes the Data collected to track and examine the use of this website, to prepare reports on its activities and share them with other Google services. Google may use the Data collected to contextualize and personalize the ads of its own advertising network.

Google ReCaptcha and Cloudflare Protection

Google ReCaptcha is a cyber security protection system provided by Google LLC that secures this website and a variety of forms, including our contact form, newsletter sign up form and registration or login pages of Keasbey Services. Cloudflare is a cyber security protection service that monitors traffic of this website in order to keep Keasbey Services secure. Both Google ReCaptcha as well as Cloudflare analyze traffic of this website and other Keasbey Services and might analyze user data.

Your personal rights

Many privacy laws give rights to individuals over their personal data. These laws include the General Data Protection Regulation, or 'GDPR'. In the following sections the Policy will outline what your rights are according to the GDPR and how Keasbey Company complies with these regulations.

Be informed

You have the right to be informed about the personal data Keasbey Company stores about you and how Keasbey Company processes this data. Keasbey Company engages in a couple of ways of how the User is getting informed about his or her personal data. This includes:

  • the Privacy Policy outlined in this document
  • answers to specific requests via our contact form

Access

You have the right to request access to your personal data based on Article 15 of the GDPR. There are two ways of how you can access your personal data.

  • As part of Keasbey Services, in which you can sign up and manage your own account data you can download your data in the profile section of the User Area.
  • You can file a request via our contact form

Rectification

The User has the right to update his or her personal data or file a request to amend or update your personal information, if something is inaccurate or incomplete. You can update your information either by

  • updating your personal information on your profile section on websites and Keasbey Services that require a user account or
  • by filing a request to update your information via our contact form

Erase

You have the right to request that we erase your personal data. Usually, we erase all your personal data. There are a few cases, in which Keasbey Company is unable to delete your data. For instance, if there is a legal obligation to keep your data or if the data is necessary to prevent fraud. Another case could be data necessary to resolve legal conflicts. In order to file a request you can use our contact form.

Other Rights

In addition to these rights described above you have the right to restrict, object, portability, not to be subject of automated decision making, to withdrawal from consent or the right to file a complain. You can restrict your data if your personal data is inaccurate, our processing is unlawful or if we do not need your personal data for a specific purpose. In order to file a request in regard to these rights you can use our contact form.

How we keep your personal data safe

Keasbey is committed to protecting our users' personal data. We put in place appropriate technical and organisational measures to help protect the security of your personal data. However, be aware that no system is ever completely secure. We have put various safeguards in place to guard against unauthorised access and unnecessary retention of personal data in our systems. To protect your user account, we encourage you to:

  • use a strong password which you only use for your Keasbey account
  • never share your password with anyone
  • limit access to your computer and browser
  • log out once you have finished using the Keasbey Services on a shared device

If other individuals have access to your Keasbey account, then they can access personal data, controls and the Keasbey Service available in your account. For example, you might have allowed someone to use your account on a shared device. It's your responsibility to only allow individuals to use your account where you're comfortable sharing this personal data with them. Anyone else's use of your Keasbey account may impact your personalised recommendations and be included in your data download.
